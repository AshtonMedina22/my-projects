Augmented LLM app

Training Manual Creator with Vercel-native tool calling.

A browser-based LLM app backed by a Vercel serverless API. It can read, write, revise, delete, and enrich employee training manual sections with verified contact lookup.

Open live app Project brief
4 LLM-callable tools

Tool architecture

01

The assistant reads manual sections before summarizing or revising them.

02

03

Contact lookup avoids invented HR, IT, finance, safety, and facilities details.

04

Delete actions are described as confirmation-sensitive destructive operations.
