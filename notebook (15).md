
<details><summary style="display:list-item; font-size:16px; color:blue;"> Examining the tokens! </summary>

Examine the tokens generated by Wordpiece. Note how more commonly occurring words such as "is", "the" and even "soul" are preserved intact whereas the less common word "brevity" is broken into subwords. This makes sense if you think about the training corpus of a tokenizer and the frequency of words occurring. 
    
The biggest advantage of subword tokenization is that it can account for words that may not be in the training corpus, i.e., the so-called "rare words", in this manner.
